Array Method (List Method)

  • Push , Pop
    • colors.push("green")
    • colors.pop()
    • Use pop to remove the last item in array
  • Shift , Unshift
    • colors.unshift("green")
    • Use unshift to add to the front of an array!
    • colors.shift()
    • Use shift to remove the first item
  • IndexOf
    • colors.indexOf("green")
    • 없을때 -1 , 2개 있을 때 앞에꺼.
  • Slice
    • var two = colors.slice(1,3)
    • colors의 1과 2로 구성된 array인 two 생성.
